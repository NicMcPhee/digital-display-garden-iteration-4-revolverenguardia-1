Welcome to the Digital Display Garden

For the WCROC Horticultural Garden

If you got here through scanning QR codes...

  • First you will see a list of flowers in the bed corresponding to the QR code you read.
  • You can select a flower in order to leave feedback on a plant you see in the garden.
    Any feedback is valuable so please share!
  • Press 'Back' to go back to a bed and view other flowers.
  • It is possible to select another bed by ######REDACTED######.
  • Otherwise, scanning more QR codes will continue to bring you straight to the bed that you scanned.

If you got here in your web browser...

  • Click 'Garden' at the top of the page to view a list of beds in the Garden.
  • Once there click on a bed to view the flowers within it.
